Pickerington High School North Robotics

The PHSN robotics club participates in the VEX Robotics competiton, where the students learn to build and program their robots to compete in multiple competitions throughout the year.

We usually have 4-5 teams that compete in 3-4 competitons throughout the year.

This a group of very motivated students who enjoy sharing their passion for creating, whether you are interested in programming, building, designing, or just to have fun, there is something for everyone.

How to Join:

Talk to Mr. Good or Mr. Moultrie to join the club!
