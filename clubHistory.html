Our Club's History

The PHSN robotics club began in 2017 where we had one team, and since then the club has grown much larger with each year having 4-5 teams, and a much more diverse group of people. Our teams have competed in competitons all across Ohio.

Our club began very small, and we didn't have access to a practice feild. All of our programming was done at the competitions, but in our 2019-2020 season, we finally built a field in the back of the North News Network room.

Our teams have been working hard recently, now that the club is more developed, to try to qualify for states. We still have yet to reach this goal, but we will keep pushing forward to reach it!
