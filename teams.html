Our Teams

For our 2021-2022 season, our school had four teams. Each team worked very hard to complete their robots, and they did a great job!

Click on the team names below to learn more about each specific team


Jared - 14760A

This team's robot can park on the ramp with 3 towers (a rare skill in this competition). Team 14760A can also score a win point for both sides with their autonomous code.


Vlad - 14760B

This team's robot can hold 2 towers, and is able to stack towers on the ramp. They can also unscore towers on the opposing ramp. Team 14760B can also score a win point for both sides with their autonomous code.


Rutheford - 14760C

This team's robot can hold 1 tower and can stack on the balance. Team 14760C can also score a win point for both sides with their autonomous code.


Little Lad - 14760P

This team's robot can hold 2 towers and park on the ramp. Team 14760P can also score a win point for both sides with their autonomous code.
